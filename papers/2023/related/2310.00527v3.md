We present Contextualized Local Visual Embeddings (CLoVE), a self-supervised convolutional-based method that learns representations suited for dense prediction tasks. CLoVE deviates from current methods and optimizes a single loss function that operates at the level of contextualized local embeddings learned from output feature maps of convolution neural network (CNN) encoders.
