**一句话概括**
In the case of a D-dimensional non-Abelian 1-form gauge theory (without any interaction with the matter fields), we show that the application of the Noether theorem does not lead to the derivations of the Becchi-Rouet-Stora-Tyutin (BRST) and anti-BRST charges that obey (i) the (anti-)BRST invariance, and (ii) the nilpotency property (despite the fact that these charges are derived from the infinitesimal, continuous and nilpotent (anti-)BRST symmetry transformations).
