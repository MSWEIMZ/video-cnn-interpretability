**一句话概括**
Inspired by the recent discovery of metallic spin supersolidity and its giant magnetocaloric effect in the rare-earth alloy EuCo$_2$Al$_9$ [Nature 651, 61 (2026)], we perform a combined study through electronic structure analysis, effective spin model, and Monte Carlo simulations on a stacked triangular lattice, and reveal a novel mechanism for the emergence of 3D spin supersolid in a metallic antiferromagnet.
