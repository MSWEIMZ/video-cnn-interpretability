#### 核心思想
This technical report presents our approach "Knights" to solve the action recognition task on a small subset of Kinetics-400 i.e. Kinetics400ViPriors without using any extra-data. Our approach has 3 main components: state-of-the-art Temporal Contrastive self-supervised pretraining, video transformer models, and optical flow modality.
