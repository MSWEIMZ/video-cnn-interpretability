### 📖 方法论

#### 核心思想
This paper presents a novel method to determine rate-distortion optimized transform coefficients for efficient compression of videos generated from point clouds. The method exploits a generalized frequency selective extrapolation approach that iteratively determines rate-distortion-optimized coefficients for all basis functions of two-dimensional discrete cosine and sine transforms.
